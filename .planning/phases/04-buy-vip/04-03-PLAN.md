# PayTR get-token (RESEARCH.md)
- POST to https://www.paytr.com/odeme/api/get-token with form body. Do NOT send merchant_key or merchant_salt in the body.
- paytr_token = buildPaytrToken(params) with hash_str = merchant_id + user_ip + merchant_oid + email + payment_amount + user_basket + no_installment + max_installment + currency + test_mode; message = hash_str + merchant_salt; HMAC-SHA256 with merchant_key, base64.
- user_basket: base64(JSON.stringify([["VIP 1 Ay", "99.00", 1], ...])) — unit price as string, quantity number.
- payment_amount in kuruş (integer). currency = "TL". test_mode from PAYTR_TEST_MODE. user_ip: from request (X-Forwarded-For or similar); RESEARCH warns localhost causes "Geçersiz paytr_token" in dev.
- Iframe: src = https://www.paytr.com/odeme/guvenli/{token}. Load script https://www.paytr.com/js/iframeResizer.min.js?v2 and call iFrameResize({}, '#paytriframe'). Use iframe_v2: 1 in get-token POST.
</context>
